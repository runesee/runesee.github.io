Middle Kingdom

Middle Kingdom is a mod I am developing for the video game Hearts of Iron IV. HoI4 is a grand strategy game, taking place in the 20th century. In an attempt to push the limitations of the game engine, and because I personally take interest in ancient history, this mod instead takes place from 2010 - 1995 BC. Peasant Rebellion

Peasant Rebellion is a spin-off of the classic Atari game Space Invaders. There are starving peasants approaching in waves trying to attack you by shooting arrows. The goal of the game is to stay alive as long as possible, killing peasants and getting the highest possible score. The game ends when the player loses all their lives.
