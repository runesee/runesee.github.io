Tidepool

Tidepool is a game where you control a single cell in a 30x30 grid. You can move your character using the arrow keys on your keyboard. Holding left ctrl while moving makes your characther move 5 cells at once. Walls spawn in waves from random directions, and your goal is to navigate your character through holes in these walls. Getting hit by a wall loses you the game. Try to survive for as long as possible. Good luck!
